Work About

Headphone

Timeline 2 weeks Tools Solidworks & Keyshot
Context
This project reimagines the traditional headphone as a strong visual statement. Using SolidWorks, I developed a squared form with a mosaic pearl inspired surface to create a bold and sculptural look. The goal was to shift headphones from a simple tech accessory to a visually expressive object. Final renders in KeyShot highlight the materials, lighting, and finish to emphasize its aesthetic impact.
Material Description
Outer Shell: CNC machined aluminum with custom pearlescent anodized finish Headband Frame: Polished stainless steel
Canopy: Knit mesh textile Ear Cushions: Memory foam with soft woven fabric cover

Mood Board

Material, form, and light references: sculptural jewelry, translucent panels, iridescent pearl surfaces, and glass.
